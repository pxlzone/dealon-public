About Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the website recognise your device and remember information about your visit, such as your preferences and login status.

DealOn uses cookies and similar technologies on this website and the DealOn platform (together, the “Site”). This Cookie Policy explains what cookies we use, why we use them, and how you can control them.

Legal Framework

Our use of cookies is governed by the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations 2003 (PECR) and UK GDPR. Under PECR, we must:

  • Obtain your consent before setting non-essential cookies on your device; and
  • Provide clear information about what each cookie does.

Strictly necessary cookies (those essential for the Site to function) do not require consent. All other cookies require your opt-in consent, which you can provide or withdraw through our cookie consent banner.

Your Cookie Choices

Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit the Site, you will see a cookie consent banner that allows you to:

  • Accept all cookies: All categories of cookies will be set on your device.
  • Accept necessary only: Only strictly necessary cookies will be set. Functional and analytics cookies will not be used.
  • Customise: You can select which categories of non-essential cookies you wish to accept.

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the “Cookie Settings” link in the Site footer.

Browser Settings

You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. However, if you block strictly necessary cookies, parts of the Site may not function correctly.

For information on how to manage cookies in common browsers, visit your browser’s privacy or cookies settings (for example, Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge).

Opt-Out of Analytics

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking across all websites by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our pages. We do not control these cookies. The third parties listed in the cookie tables above have their own privacy and cookie policies, which we encourage you to review.

DealOn does not use cookies for third-party advertising or behavioural tracking. We do not sell cookie data to third parties.
