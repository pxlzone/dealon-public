AI Disclosure Notice

Last updated: June 15, 2026

This AI Disclosure Notice explains how DEALON LTD (“DealOn”, “we”, “us”) uses artificial intelligence and related automated technologies in connection with this website and our M&A advisory services. It should be read together with our Privacy Policy, Cookie Policy, and Platform Terms of Use.

Purpose of This Notice

DealOn describes itself as an AI-native M&A advisory. We use software, machine learning, and related techniques to support parts of our workflow. This notice is intended to help you understand where AI may be involved, what it is — and is not — used for, and the role of human advisors in our process.

Scope

This notice applies to:

  • Information, demonstrations, and interactive features on this website;
  • AI-assisted tools and workflows used in the course of sell-side and buy-side advisory engagements, where applicable; and
  • Outputs, scores, rankings, summaries, or suggestions generated or assisted by automated systems made available through DealOn.

How We May Use AI

Depending on the service and stage of a transaction, we may use AI or automated systems to support activities such as:

  • Buyer and seller matching: analysing company profiles, sector data, and compatibility factors to identify potential counterparties;
  • Valuation and market intelligence: benchmarking against comparable transactions, multiples, and market signals;
  • Document processing: extracting, classifying, or summarising information from materials you or counterparties provide;
  • Workflow support: organising deal stages, reminders, and internal review queues; and
  • Website functionality: improving site performance, personalising content presentation, and analysing aggregated usage patterns.

The specific systems and methods used may change over time as our technology and service offering evolve.

Human Oversight

DealOn is not a fully automated decision-making service. Material advisory judgments — including whether to proceed with a mandate, which parties to approach, how to position a business for sale, valuation conclusions presented to clients, and negotiation strategy — are made or validated by qualified human advisors.

AI-generated or AI-assisted outputs are intended to inform and accelerate human review. They do not replace professional judgment, regulatory obligations, or client-specific due diligence.

Limitations and Accuracy

AI systems can produce incomplete, outdated, or incorrect results. Outputs may reflect limitations in source data, model design, or assumptions built into the system. You should not rely on any automated output as the sole basis for a financial, legal, tax, or commercial decision.

We do not guarantee the accuracy, completeness, timeliness, or fitness for purpose of any AI-assisted output. Independent verification and professional advice remain essential.

Not Financial Advice or a Regulated Service

DEALON LTD is not authorised or regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority. Content on this website, and any AI-assisted materials or summaries we provide, are for general information only. They do not constitute financial, investment, legal, or tax advice, nor an offer or solicitation to buy or sell any security or business.

Your Information and AI Processing

Where personal data or confidential business information is processed using automated tools, we handle it in accordance with our Privacy Policy and applicable data protection law. We apply access controls, contractual safeguards, and internal policies designed to limit use of client data to legitimate advisory and operational purposes.

Please do not submit information through this website that you are not authorised to share, or that you consider highly sensitive unless we have agreed appropriate handling arrangements with you.

Third-Party AI and Infrastructure Providers

We may use third-party software, cloud infrastructure, or model providers to deliver parts of our platform. Where those providers process data on our behalf, we seek to use reputable vendors and appropriate contractual protections. Third-party services may be subject to their own terms and privacy practices.

Intellectual Property and Outputs

Unless otherwise agreed in writing, DealOn retains rights in its platform, models, prompts, workflows, and proprietary methodologies. AI-assisted outputs prepared for a specific client engagement are provided for that client’s internal use in connection with the relevant mandate, subject to any separate engagement letter or terms agreed between the parties.

Illustrative and Marketing Content

Some figures, timelines, match scores, or examples shown on this website or in marketing materials are illustrative or based on aggregated, anonymised, or hypothetical data. They are not promises of future performance, deal outcomes, or specific results for your business.

Changes to This Notice

We may update this AI Disclosure Notice from time to time to reflect changes in technology, regulation, or our services. Material updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
