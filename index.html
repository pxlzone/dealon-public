The DealOn Difference

Four reasons business owners choose us

01
Near-zero upfront cost — success-based fees
Unlike most EMEA advisors who charge retainers of £10,000–£50,000 before even beginning work, DealOn charges just a nominal £1,000 retainer to secure your mandate — no monthly charges, no interim milestone fees. Our success fee is only triggered upon legal completion of the deal. This is our commitment that we are invested in your outcome, not your engagement letter.
02
AI-powered matching finds buyers others can't
Our proprietary matching engine scans and scores thousands of potential acquirers against your company profile in real-time. By analysing financial fit, strategic synergy, acquisition history, and sector dynamics, we surface high-probability matches that manual processes systematically miss — expanding your buyer universe dramatically.
03
City-calibre advisors, not junior associates
Your deal is led by senior professionals with direct experience at firms like Rothschild, Lazard, KPMG, and Goldman Sachs. At DealOn, senior people run deals — not supervise them from a distance. You get a named lead advisor who is accountable from mandate to completion, supported by our AI infrastructure.
04
Close in weeks, not years
The EMEA mid-market average is 9–12 months from mandate to completion. DealOn's combination of AI-driven buyer identification, parallel processing, and structured deal acceleration has brought our average down to 68 days, with the fastest closing in just 49 days. Speed protects value and reduces deal fatigue for both parties.
